Mayor / Council

The Mayor and Council form the cornerstone of municipal governance, providing both executive leadership and legislative oversight. The Mayor serves as the chief executive officer, responsible for implementing policies, overseeing city departments, and ensuring that municipal operations are conducted efficiently and in accordance with law. The Council functions as the legislative body, representing the community’s diverse districts and creating ordinances, resolutions, and policies that guide local development, public safety, and community welfare. Together, the Mayor and Council maintain a system of checks and balances. The Mayor proposes initiatives, manages budgets, and supervises departmental activities, while the Council evaluates proposals, approves budgets, and ensures that constituent interests are represented. Public engagement is integral, with residents encouraged to participate through council meetings, hearings, surveys, and town halls.
